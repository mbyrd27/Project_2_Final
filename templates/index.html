What is popular near you?

Our group has collected over 36,000 records of radio station song plays in 25 cities across the United States. The data was pulled from a "FiveThirtyEight" GitHub repo which housed data behind the story, "Classic Rock Isn't What it Used to Be." The "Now Playing" list was scraped from the website of each Classic Rock station over a week's period in June, 2014. Each record pertains to one song play.

Below you can get a glimpse of what artist was popular in each city. Simply click on the map icon of your city of interest and view the broad information. Click the "Other Data" link to get a more in depth view about what artists were more popular where, a song breakdown, and what time of day each rock artist was played the most.
